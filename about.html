ABOUT DURA TECH

Built by people who've sat on the other side of the procurement table.

Dura Tech was founded by engineers and policy staff who spent years inside government IT — and got tired of watching vendors disappear after launch day.

OUR MISSION

Public infrastructure shouldn't depend on who's still on the payroll.

Most software vendors are built to win the next contract. We're built to make sure the system we shipped five years ago still runs cleanly today — documented, maintainable, and owned by the agency that needs it, not locked to us.

That means slower sales cycles and harder engineering. It also means agencies that work with us once tend to come back for the next system, and the one after that.

HOW WE GOT HERE

A timeline shaped by real agency work
