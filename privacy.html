1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, work email, agency affiliation, and message content when you submit a contact or briefing request form. We also collect limited technical information automatically, including browser type, IP address, and pages visited, to maintain site security and performance.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to briefing requests, procurement questions, and general inquiries
  • Maintain and secure our website and systems
  • Understand aggregate site usage to improve content and navigation
  • Comply with applicable legal and contractual obligations

3. Data sharing & disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who support our website operations under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law, subpoena, or government request, or to protect the rights and safety of Dura Tech, our clients, or the public.

4. Data security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information we handle, consistent with the same security standards we build into client systems. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below; we will respond within a reasonable timeframe consistent with applicable law.

6. Cookies & tracking

This site uses limited cookies necessary for basic functionality and aggregated analytics. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect some site features.

7. Children's privacy

This website is intended for government, agency, and business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and any such information identified will be deleted.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@duratech.com or by mail to 1100 Continuity Avenue NW, Suite 400, Washington, D.C. 20005.
